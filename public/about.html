ABOUT US

Our Mission

Voluntree is a dedicated volunteer organization committed to making a positive impact on communities and the environment.


Our Values

  1. Sustainability: We prioritize long-term ecological health in all our projects.
  2. Community: We believe in the power of collective effort and people are at the heart of what we do.
  3. Integrity: We uphold transparency and honesty in our operations.

Our People

Our volunteers and staff are the heart of Voluntree. We come from a divere background of software engineers, data scientists and web developers. Together, we are passionate about supporting our volunteers and branches to make a difference one step at a time.
